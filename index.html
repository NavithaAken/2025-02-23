Neuroplasticy : How the brain adpts and change

Neuroplasticity is the brain's ability to change and adapt throughout life. It's also known as neural plasticity or plasticity.

How Neuroplasticity works
  • The brain's neural networks can grow, reorganize, or change their functions
  • The brain can reprogram itself to adapt to new situations and environments
  • The brain can relearn after injuries like a stroke or traumatic brain injury

Mental Health

Mental health is a state of mental well-being that enables people to cope with the stresses of life, realize their abilities, learn well and work well, and contribute to their community. It is an integral component of health and well-being that underpins our individual and collective abilities to make decisions, build relationships and shape the world we live in.
